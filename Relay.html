12v Relay Basics

What is a relay?

A relay is very powerful, useful and common component in 12 automotive electronics. A relay is essentially a switch, the switch allows higher amperage to pass through the circuit and takes the stress off the user operated switch inside the vehicle. Utilizing relays when adding accessories will not only greatly increase the safety factor of your wiring, but as you become accustomed to their use they will make the wiring job go much smoother.
